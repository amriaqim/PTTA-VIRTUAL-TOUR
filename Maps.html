Find Your Way Around

Navigate every floor of Perpustakaan Tunku Tun Aminah — rooms, facilities, and directions all in one place.

Campus Location

Floor Directory

Aras 1

Recreation Space

Free relaxation area with games & light gym equipment. Open Mon–Fri, 9am–5pm.

Closed Reference Room

Houses 10,000+ UTHM theses for on-site reference. Capacity: 50 users.

Aras 2

Sister Zone

Women-only rest and wellness space with sofas, bean bags, and carpeted flooring.

Hikmah Room

Private study rooms for 4–6 users. Select rooms have computers & LCD projectors.

Iqra Room

118 individual study rooms across Level 2 & 4. Available at RM20/month.

Remote Pilot Smart Classroom

Modern smart learning space with structured and relaxed seating zones.

AI Lab Room

Smart collaboration space for virtual meetings & training. Capacity: 15 users.

Aras 3

Makerspace

Creative hub for workshops, robotics, and hands-on innovative projects.

The Interactive Learning Space

Digital workspace with 66 workstations powered by VDI technology.

Inspire Lab

Lounge-style study space with 18 laptops, printer, Wi-Fi & power outlets.

Lestari Room 1

Seminar & conference room with long tables and LCD projector. Capacity: 50.

Lestari Room 2

Collaborative seminar room with round-table layout and LCD projector. Capacity: 50.

Al-Jazari Auditorium

Main auditorium for lectures, conferences & events. Capacity: 150 users.

Lestari Lounge

Dining area for library users to take a break and have meals.

Eksplorasi Room

Group discussion rooms for 6–12 users. Select rooms have computers & LCD projectors.

Aras 4

Serambi Al-Quran

Quiet spiritual space for Quranic reading, prayer & reflection. Free for all users.
